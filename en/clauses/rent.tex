\section{Rent}
\label{sec:rent}

\subsection{Amount}
\label{sec:amount}

Tenant agrees to pay to Landlord a monthly rent in the amount of \LeaseAmount{}. Rent shall be due and payable in advance on or before the \LeaseDueDate{} day of each calendar month during the term of this Agreement, without demand or notice.

\subsection{Payment method}
\label{sec:paymentmethod}

Rent shall be paid by one or more of the following methods as approved by the Landlord: 
\begin{itemize}
  \item[$\square$] Cash
  \item[$\square$] Check
  \item[$\square$] Bank Transfer
  \item[$\square$] Electronic Payment Platform
  \item[$\square$] Other (Specify)
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Late Fee}
\label{sec:latefee}

If rent is not received by the Landlord within \LateFeeGracePeriod{} days after the due date, Tenant shall be considered in default and shall be required to pay a late fee in the amount of \LateFeeAmount{}, to the extent permitted by applicable law. Acceptance of late rent or late fees shall not constitute a waiver of the Landlord’s right to require timely payment in the future.
