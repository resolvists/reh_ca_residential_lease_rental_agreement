\section{Term of Lease}
\label{sec:termoflease}

\subsection{Fixed-Term Lease}
\label{sec:fixedterm}
The term of this Lease shall commence on \LeaseStartDate{} and shall continue through \LeaseEndDate{}. The Lease shall automatically terminate at the end of the Lease Term unless extended by mutual written agreement of the parties.

\subsection{Month-to-Month Lease}
\label{sec:fixedterm}
The term of this Lease shall commence on \LeaseStartDate{} and shall continue on a month-to-month basis until terminated by either party upon written notice as required by applicable law.
